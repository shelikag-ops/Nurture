## Values Integration in Science
The science curriculum is a natural vehicle for all three pillars:

- **Dharma** — "What is the right thing to do with this knowledge?" (e.g., when studying energy: how do we use it responsibly?)
- **Seva** — Connecting to real-world impact: "Who benefits when scientists understand this?" Ahimsa weaves in naturally through B-strand (respecting all living things, food chains, ecosystems)
- **Gratitude** — End-of-chapter reflection: "Name one thing about nature that amazes you." The wonder of science IS a gratitude practice

Each web app should include a short "Wonder Moment" slide — one awe-inspiring fact or image that invites her to stop and appreciate rather than just process.
